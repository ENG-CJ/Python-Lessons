waxaa yaraanaaya cillad bixinta program-kaaga
waxaase kale oo kordhaya Readable Program-kaaga in si fican loo fahmi karo

Modules-ka aad bay muhiim uyihiin marka lajoogo Developing-ka

si function-kaas ugu shaqeeyaan another file
